Eyes Horror & Coop Multiplayer


Eyes Horror & Coop Multiplayer is The Horror Game (Called Eyes - Scary & Creepy Survival Horror Game in the Play Store) is a free-roaming horror game setting around a mansion, where a thief is to collect 20 (Or 8, 12 or 30) money bags. While doing so, the player will be chased by a monster, as there are rumors of hauntings in the mansion.
