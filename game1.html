Moblie Legend


Mobile Legend is RPG game also battling with team with other players.

It is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile phones. The game is free-to-play and is only monetized through in-game purchases like characters and skins. Each player can control a selectable character, called a Hero, with unique abilities and traits.

You can get it in Google and Play store.
